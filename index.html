WHAT IS DESERT PIONEERS?

Desert Pioneers is a fast-paced resource collection and settlement race for 2-6 people.

Each player is given a task that they must complete by collecting resources and building settlements throughout the desert. If they can complete their task and return to the Desert City first, they can claim the land for their monarch, but watch out because the competition is vicious and will do whatever it takes to win.
